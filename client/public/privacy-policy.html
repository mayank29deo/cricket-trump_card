Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Cricket TrumpCard ("we", "our", "the app") is a free multiplayer cricket card game available on web and Android. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

  • Display Name: The nickname you enter when creating or joining a game room. This is not linked to any personal identity.
  • Authentication: If you sign in via Google on the web app, we receive your name and email from Google. We use this solely to identify your account.
  • Game Data: Room codes, game state, scores, and card selections are processed in real-time on our servers and are not permanently stored after the game ends.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdSense: Our web app displays ads via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing activity. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Used for authentication on the web app. See Supabase Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Game sessions are temporary and not stored after completion. Account data (if signed in) is retained until you request deletion.

Children's Privacy

Cricket TrumpCard is a card game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require an account to play on mobile.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account data by contacting us. Since game data is temporary, there is nothing to delete after a session ends.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, reach out via our website: trumpyard.com
